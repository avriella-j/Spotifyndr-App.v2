Frequently Asked Questions

Click "Sign in with Spotify" on the landing page. You'll be redirected to Spotify's authorisation page where you can grant Spotifyndr access to your music profile.

We access your top artists, top tracks, and recently played music to find users with similar taste. We never access your password or payment information.

You can revoke access at any time through your Spotify account settings under "Apps". This won't delete your Spotifyndr account but will stop data syncing.

We use machine learning algorithms (KNN similarity and logistic regression) to analyse your music profile and find users with similar taste. The more you use the app, the better recommendations become.
